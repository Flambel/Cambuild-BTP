Who are we?

To be a creator of perspectives such is the ambition of the company CAMBUILD-BTP since its creation. Today, it is one of the leading Cameroonian construction industry players and stands out for the technical nature of the buildings produced. All of our employees cultivate the same culture of excellence, respect for commitments and proximity to our private, public or institutional clients.
Together, we continue to create new perspectives, capture new markets, design lasting solutions to all constraints.

Production and sale of concrete blocks and slabs

We have an industrial factory of materials for our own sites but also for sale for colleagues.
